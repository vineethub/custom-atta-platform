# Personalized Atta Platform

## Overview

Personalized Atta Platform is a Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) web application that allows customers to create customized atta blends based on their nutritional requirements, dietary preferences, and health goals.

Unlike traditional atta brands that offer fixed recipes, the platform enables customers to select grains, define blend percentages, view nutritional information, and order freshly ground atta delivered to their doorstep.

## Vision

To become India's leading personalized nutrition flour brand by enabling every household to create atta tailored to their unique health needs.

## Problem Statement

Consumers currently have limited control over the composition of their atta.

Different individuals have different nutritional requirements:

* Fitness enthusiasts need high-protein blends.
* Diabetic individuals require low glycemic combinations.
* Senior citizens benefit from high-fiber blends.
* Families often prefer customized grain combinations.

Current market offerings provide fixed recipes with little personalization.

## Solution

A platform that allows customers to:

* Select grains
* Customize blend percentages
* View nutrition information
* Calculate pricing instantly
* Order freshly prepared atta online
