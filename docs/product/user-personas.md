# User Personas

## Persona 1: Fitness Enthusiast

### Profile

* Age: 18-35
* Regular gym attendee
* Focused on muscle gain and fitness

### Goals

* Increase protein intake
* Improve nutrition
* Healthy alternatives to regular atta

### Pain Points

* Standard atta lacks protein
* Limited healthy flour options
* No customization available

### Expected Blend

* Wheat
* Chana
* Soybean
* Oats

---

## Persona 2: Health Conscious Family

### Profile

* Age: 28-50
* Family with children
* Interested in healthier food options

### Goals

* Better nutrition
* Balanced diet
* Trusted ingredients

### Pain Points

* Doesn't know what grains are included in packaged atta
* Wants transparency

### Expected Blend

* Wheat
* Ragi
* Jowar
* Oats

---

## Persona 3: Diabetic Consumer

### Profile

* Age: 35-65

### Goals

* Manage blood sugar
* Improve overall health

### Pain Points

* Difficult to find suitable flour blends
* Limited control over ingredients

### Expected Blend

* Barley
* Chana
* Oats
* Low wheat composition

---

## Persona 4: Senior Citizen

### Profile

* Age: 55+

### Goals

* High fiber diet
* Easy digestion

### Pain Points

* Digestive issues
* Fixed market blends don't meet needs

### Expected Blend

* Barley
* Oats
* Ragi
* Wheat
