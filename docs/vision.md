# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

Enable every household to consume healthier and more personalized nutrition through customized atta blends.

## Mission

Provide fresh, transparent, and customizable flour solutions that meet the nutritional needs of modern consumers.

## Long-Term Goal

Become India's most trusted personalized flour brand.

## Success Metrics

### Year 1

* 500 customers
* 100 recurring customers
* Delhi NCR operations

### Year 2

* 5,000 customers
* Subscription model launch
* Multi-city delivery

### Year 3

* AI nutrition engine
* Mobile applications
* Pan-India presence

## Core Principles

### Personalization First

Customers should have complete control over their atta composition.

### Transparency

Every ingredient, percentage, and nutritional value must be visible.

### Freshness

Atta should be prepared after order placement whenever operationally possible.

### Simplicity

The customization process should remain simple and beginner-friendly.
